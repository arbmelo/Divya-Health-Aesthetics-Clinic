What it is

Laser hair reduction sessions.

Common areas include face, underarms, arms, legs, and body areas.

Who it is for

  • Unwanted hair.
  • Regular waxing, shaving, or threading.
  • Ingrown hair concerns.

Procedure flow

  1. Consultation: skin type, hair texture, and treatment area.
  2. Preparation: the area is cleaned and marked; protective eyewear is used.
  3. Session: laser hair reduction treatment.
  4. Aftercare: aftercare and next-session timing.

Sessions and aftercare

Multiple sessions are usually needed. Avoid waxing or plucking before sessions. Follow aftercare advice.

FAQs

It depends on area, hair thickness, skin type, and growth cycle.
Shaving is usually preferred over waxing or plucking between sessions.
Mild redness or warmth can happen. Follow aftercare advice.
